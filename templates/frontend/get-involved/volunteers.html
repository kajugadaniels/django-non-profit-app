Volunteers

Became a volunteer

Volunteers are crucial to AOF.

When you volunteer with AOF, you make transformation possible and bring hope to families and communities throughout Rwanda.

Whether you serve once a week, once a month, or once a year, your volunteered time allows us to serve Rwandans more effectively. There are numerous ways you can make a difference!

Some of our Volunteers Needs

Connecting CW with local churches and events in your area

Being an advocate/spokeperson in your community/region to help share the message of helping children in need around the world

Running a booth and handing out materials at events

Processing donations, and payment information at events

Helping to secure new child sponsorship

Host a sponsorship Sunday at your church
