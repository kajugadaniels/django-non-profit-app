Community Empowerment.

The Ministry fosters spiritual growth activities and Bible studies in several villages in Rwanda Every initiative undertaken by the Africa Oasis Foundation, be it educational scholarships, vocational training programs, or medical care services, is firmly rooted in a biblical worldview. This perspective not only guides our actions but also places a strong emphasis on fostering meaningful relationships and promoting disciple-making. By integrating these core principles, we ensure that our outreach efforts are holistic, impactful, and aligned with our foundational values.

While addressing the physical needs of the communities we serve is a vital aspect of our mission, it is not our ultimate objective. We strive to nurture them with love and compassion, aiming to provide not only physical sustenance but also spiritual nourishment. Our deepest desire is to see individuals spiritually fed and restored to a right relationship with God. Through this holistic approach, we seek to foster enduring transformation and growth, grounded in love and faith.

At the heart of our outreach efforts lies a profound commitment to answering Jesus' call to go and make disciples. For our team, each interaction is more than a service; it's an opportunity to make a lasting impact on the lives we touch. The mentors in our Sponsorship Program and the Family Mentors at Oasis Christian Academy embody this mission. They go beyond the conventional role of social workers, immersing themselves in the lives of the children and families they serve. Their genuine love and dedication are evident in their daily actions, as they provide not only guidance but also heartfelt discipleship. Through their unwavering support and mentorship, we aim to foster deep, meaningful relationships that lead to spiritual growth and lasting transformation. Our ultimate goal is to see each individual we serve grow in their faith and develop a strong, enduring relationship with God.

As you delve deeper into understanding the work of the Africa Oasis Foundation in Rwanda, we hope you clearly perceive our unwavering commitment to the call of discipleship in both our actions and methodologies. We believe that the Bible outlines a model of caring for others that is deeply rooted in relationships, mirroring the discipleship model of Jesus Christ. This model is achievable only through the grace and guidance of Jesus Christ himself. Our mission is far from complete, and there remains much to be done in service to our great God. We are dedicated to serving in a manner that not only meets immediate needs but also inspires and encourages others to follow the path of the way, the truth, and the life. By fostering meaningful relationships and emphasizing spiritual growth, we strive to build a community that reflects the love and teachings of Jesus Christ, ensuring that our work has a lasting, transformative impact.
