Africa Oasis Foundation sponsors over 100 vulnerable children to a Christian school through an educational sponsorship: these children receive three meals a day, school Supplies, medical care, and spiritual discipleship. They learn about Jesus in school, and on Saturdays all the sponsored children come to the organization fellowship site for bible study, worship, lunch, and playing. They are sent home with food and soap every Saturday, along with seeds during the rainy season so that they and their families may begin growing their own food.
