Feeding Outreach

Many communities face the challenges of malnutrition and starvation. Each month, we provide lunch to up to 1,500 students and children in Bugesera who otherwise cannot afford to attend school. Our aim is to draw more children off the streets and back into the classroom, while also alleviating widespread hunger in the community. By offering these meals, we not only address immediate nutritional needs but also encourage educational participation and long-term personal development.
