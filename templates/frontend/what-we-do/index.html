The Community Impact Programs of Africa Oasis Foundation: Rooted in Biblical Principles for Lasting Change

01

Oasis Christian Academy

Organization is currently in the construction of the first phase of oasis Christian academy. The school will equip Rwandan students with the tools of learning and creative thinking to enable them to live fully for the glory of God.

02

Farming Outreach

In an effort to continually improve our programs and help the families we work with become more self-reliant, the Africa Oasis Foundation Farming Outreach was started in 2024. Farming God’s Way training classes are held on a bi-annual basis and are taught by our farm staff.

03

Feeding Outreach

Africa Oasis Foundation now feeds 100 children lunch every Monday through Friday. It's a heartwarming achievement that underscores our commitment to combating hunger and nourishing young minds in our community. Through dedicated efforts and the generosity of our supporters, we've expanded our feeding program to ensure that these children receive nutritious meals every weekday.

Read More

04

Education Sponsorship Program

Our Education Sponsorship Outreach aims to offer children in the Bugesera area access to education, nutrition, medical care, and discipleship that might otherwise be out of reach for them.

Read More

05

Medical Care Outreach

Africa Oasis Foundation provides physicals, HIV testing and counseling, basic hygiene education, malaria prevention, malnutrition rehabilitation, and medical financial assistance for many individuals. We work closely with our social worker in Bugesera who assesses, monitors, overseas all medical needs that arise in Bugesera.

Gospel Teaching

07

Women’s Beading / Tailoring Circle

Africa Oasis is dedicated to empowering mothers in the Bugesera District, particularly within the Kamabuye community, through our comprehensive beading and tailoring program. Each week, our Site Coordinator leads these remarkable women in Bible study sessions, fostering deep and meaningful relationships. Beyond spiritual growth, the program includes practical money management training, equipping the participants with essential skills for financial independence.
