The Mission of Africa Oasis Foundation

In the heart of Rwanda, a land known for its resilience and beauty, the Africa Oasis Foundation was established with a profound mission at its core. This foundation is dedicated to making disciples of Jesus through authentic relationships, excellent education, and strengthened communities.

Julie's Role

Julie, a devoted missionary, arrived in Rwanda, becoming a guiding light for those she encountered. Her unwavering commitment to humanitarian work, fueled by her faith, was evident in her actions

Julie's commitment to humanitarian work extended far beyond the norm. She adopted Ninah, a young Rwandan girl, into her family, providing not just shelter but also the love and guidance that Ninah needed. This act of love and sacrifice, driven by the teachings of Jesus, touched the lives of those around her.
