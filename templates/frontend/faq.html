Frequent asked questions

Are there other ways I can help my child?

Absolutely! We consistently have unique initiatives underway that would greatly benefit from additional support. We invite you to explore our website to discover how you can participate. Thank you in advance for your willingness to contribute to the remarkable endeavors at Africa Oasis Foundation.

What happens when my child graduates?

We aspire for the unique bond between you and your child to endure long after their journey with Africa Oasis Foundation has concluded. Upon your child's graduation, we will help facilitate a connection so that you may continue to stay in contact through your own arrangements.

What happens if my situation changes and I am unable to continue my sponsorship?
We recognize that life can be unpredictable, and while we greatly value your ongoing support, there may be times when sponsors need to pause their commitment due to personal situations. To ensure that our children's experience remains uninterrupted, we kindly request that you provide us with a 60-day advance notice. This allows us ample time to find a new sponsor for your child.
Will I have access to my childâ€™s family?

Rest assured that the leaders and team members of our Foundation maintain strong bonds with the families of our students and are deeply engaged in their children's education. Nonetheless, we advise against initiating personal communication with the students' parents, out of respect for cultural sensitivities and to maintain equitable treatment among all families participating in our program. Should you decide to visit the Africa Oasis Foundation and meet your sponsored child, we can facilitate a home visit for you.

Can I visit my child?

Yes! We absolutely encourage you to visit our school and meet your child.

Can I send extra gifts to my child?

We are convinced that providing our students with a superior education rooted in Christian values is the most profound gift they could receive. Moreover, you will be given the chance to contribute a specified sum every Christmas, which will be utilized to buy a present for your student on your behalf. There is also a provision for the purchase of gifts for students who have not yet been sponsored. As we strive to ensure equal educational experiences for all our children, we advise against sending extra presents to our students.

What is the expectation of my investment and my involvement?

Our program aims to cultivate Rwandan Christian leaders and entrepreneurs who will shape the world. Recognizing the significance of our mission, we trust in God's faithfulness and provision. As a sponsor, we hope you embrace the vital role of nurturing a student's growth, bolstering their educational and personal development, and enriching their lives with correspondence and prayers. We aspire for a deep, enduring bond to form between you and your student, one that flourishes well past the completion of our program.

Will I have contact with my child?

As a benefactor of the Africa Oasis Foundation, you have the opportunity to send words of support and motivation to your sponsored child up to three times annually. Our students eagerly anticipate these heartfelt messages from their sponsors. The knowledge that someone across the globe cares for them and believes in their potential greatly influences their achievements. Through our dedicated sponsor portal, you can compose and send letters filled with affection and inspiration to your child. They will receive and read your letters at school and respond in kind. Our team in the US will collect these replies and ensure they are sent to you. We organize a letter-writing event each term, providing you with the chance to correspond with your child up to three times a year. Additionally, we will keep you updated on their accomplishments and notable happenings at the school.

Are the parents involved in their childâ€™s education?

Indeed, they are committed! We hold the conviction that parents who are actively engaged and contribute to their childâ€™s educational journey tend to be more invested in their long-term academic and personal success. This commitment from our parents is evident as they either contribute towards their child's tuition or engage in our Earning and Learning Program to assist in meeting these expenses. The investment from most families is substantial, yet they recognize the value and long-term benefits associated with the high-quality education provided by the Africa Oasis Foundation. Additionally, parents are encouraged to be a part of worship assemblies, engage in parental meetings, and acquire vocational skills, further fostering a supportive community environment.

Does Africa Oasis Foundation Have an Orphanage In Rwamda ?

Africa Oasis Foundation does not operate an orphanage. Instead, our mission centers on supporting families to avert the risk of children ending up as orphans. Many times, due to an inability to meet their basic needs, parents or guardians feel compelled to place their children in orphanages. Through your partnership, the significant responsibilities of covering education fees, providing daily meals, and ensuring medical care are lifted from the shoulders of parents and guardians. This support helps keep families together.

Can I visit Africa Oasis Foundation In Rwanda

Absolutely! The Africa Oasis Foundation warmly welcomes visitors every day between 1:00 and 4:30 PM. Should you wish to pay us a visit in Rwanda, kindly fill out the visit request form. Following your submission, a member of the Africa Oasis Foundation team will be in touch. We're deeply appreciative of your interest in the Africa Oasis Foundation and eagerly anticipate your visit to Rwanda!

Can I write a letter to the children that African Oasis Foundation Serves ?

Certainly. Children in Rwanda are always thrilled to receive letters from people in Western countries. These children enjoy learning about your daily life, how you came to know about Africa Oasis Foundation, and your prayers for them as well as for those in our outreach programs. Please send your letters to our office in the United States at: Africa Oasis Foundation 9717 e Thunderhawk Rd Scottsdale , Az 85255

Is Africa Oasis Foundation?

Yes. Africa Oasis Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and your donation is tax-deductible to the fullest extent the law allows.

I have More Questions about Africa Oasis Foundation. How do I get those Answered?

If you have any more questions regarding the Africa Oasis Foundation, please feel free to send us an email at info@africanoasis.org. We will get back to you at the earliest opportunity.
