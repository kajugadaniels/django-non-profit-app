ABOUT AFRICA OASIS

Discovering God's Love: The Story of Africa Oasis Foundation in Rwanda

Africa Oasis Foundation was founded in 2021. The organization, based out of Scottsdale, Arizona, educates, empowers, and encourages the orphaned, poor and in the country of Rwanda. Africa Oasis Foundation desires to reveal the truth of God’s unconditional love through Jesus Christ to the people of Rwanda.

Sustainable Agricultural Empowerment

Farming Outreach.

In an effort to continually improve our programs and help the families we work with become more self-reliant, the Africa Oasis Foundation Farming Outreach was started in 2024. Farming God’s Way training classes are held on a bi-annual basis and are taught by our farm staff.

Nutrition for Tomorrow

Feeding Outreach.

Africa Oasis Foundation now feeds 100 children lunch every Monday through Friday. It's a heartwarming achievement that underscores our commitment to combating hunger and nourishing young minds in our community. Through dedicated efforts and the generosity of our supporters, we've expanded our feeding program to ensure that these children receive nutritious meals every weekday.
